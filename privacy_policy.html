中文版

1. 概述

感谢您使用扫雷游戏（以下简称"本应用"）。我们非常重视您的隐私保护。本隐私政策旨在帮助您了解本应用如何处理信息。

2. 信息收集

本应用本身不收集、存储或传输任何用户个人信息。

本应用是一款单机游戏，所有游戏数据（包括游戏进度、统计数据、设置偏好等）仅存储在您的设备本地，不会上传到我们的服务器。

2.1 广告服务

本应用集成了第三方广告服务以支持应用的持续开发和维护。广告服务提供商可能会收集以下信息：

  • 设备信息：设备型号、操作系统版本、唯一设备标识符（如广告ID）
  • 使用信息：应用使用情况、广告展示和点击数据
  • 位置信息：大致地理位置（基于IP地址，用于展示相关广告）

这些信息由广告服务提供商收集和处理，用于提供个性化广告和分析广告效果。我们不会直接访问或存储这些信息。

3. 数据存储

本应用使用Android系统的本地存储功能来保存以下数据：

  • 游戏统计数据：游戏次数、胜率、最佳成绩等
  • 用户设置：难度选择、是否显示过新手引导等偏好设置

这些数据仅存储在您的设备上，不会被传输或分享给任何第三方。

4. 权限说明

本应用可能请求以下Android系统权限：

4.1 网络权限

  • 权限名称：INTERNET, ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE
  • 用途：加载和展示广告内容
  • 说明：广告服务需要网络连接来获取和展示广告

4.2 存储权限（可选）

  • 用途：保存游戏统计数据和用户设置到设备本地存储
  • 说明：这些数据仅存储在您的设备上，不会上传到网络

4.3 震动权限（如果实现了震动功能）

  • 权限名称：VIBRATE
  • 用途：在特定游戏事件（如点击地雷）时提供触觉反馈
  • 说明：此权限仅用于增强游戏体验，不涉及任何数据收集

重要说明：除广告服务所需的网络权限外，本应用不会请求位置权限、相机权限、麦克风权限或联系人权限等其他敏感权限。

5. 第三方服务

本应用集成了以下第三方服务：

5.1 广告服务

本应用使用第三方广告平台来展示广告。这些广告服务提供商可能包括（但不限于）：

  • Google AdMob
  • 其他广告网络

这些服务提供商有自己的隐私政策，规定了他们如何收集和使用信息。我们建议您查看这些服务提供商的隐私政策：

  • Google隐私政策
  • Google AdMob隐私政策

5.2 广告个性化和退出

广告服务提供商可能使用您的设备广告ID来提供个性化广告。您可以通过以下方式管理广告偏好：

  • Android设备：设置 > Google > 广告 > 选择退出广告个性化
  • 重置广告ID：设置 > Google > 广告 > 重置广告ID

重要说明：除广告服务外，本应用不集成任何其他分析工具或社交媒体插件。

6. 儿童隐私

本应用适合所有年龄段用户使用。我们不会主动收集任何用户（包括儿童）的个人信息。

但是，集成的广告服务可能会收集设备信息用于展示广告。根据儿童在线隐私保护法（COPPA）和其他相关法律，我们建议家长监督儿童使用本应用。

如果您是家长并且担心广告内容，您可以在设备设置中限制广告个性化或使用家长控制功能。

7. 数据安全

本应用本身不收集或传输任何用户个人数据。所有游戏数据仅存储在您的设备本地，受您设备的安全措施保护。

对于广告服务收集的信息，这些数据由第三方广告服务提供商处理和保护。我们选择的广告服务提供商都遵循行业标准的安全措施来保护用户信息。

8. 隐私政策变更

我们可能会不定期更新本隐私政策。任何变更将通过应用更新的方式通知您。建议您定期查看本隐私政策以了解最新信息。

9. 联系我们

如果您对本隐私政策有任何疑问或建议，请通过以下方式联系我们：

邮箱：minesweeper.game.support@example.com

English Version

1. Overview

Thank you for using Minesweeper Game (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). We take your privacy very seriously. This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand how the App handles information.

2. Information Collection

The App itself does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal user information.

The App is a single-player game. All game data (including game progress, statistics, settings preferences, etc.) is stored only locally on your device and is not uploaded to our servers.

2.1 Advertising Services

The App integrates third-party advertising services to support the ongoing development and maintenance of the app. Advertising service providers may collect the following information:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers (such as advertising ID)
  • Usage Information: App usage, ad impressions and click data
  • Location Information: Approximate geographic location (based on IP address, used to display relevant ads)

This information is collected and processed by advertising service providers to provide personalized ads and analyze ad performance. We do not directly access or store this information.

3. Data Storage

The App uses Android's local storage features to save the following data:

  • Game Statistics: Number of games played, win rate, best scores, etc.
  • User Settings: Difficulty selection, tutorial display preferences, etc.

This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted or shared with any third parties.

4. Permissions

The App may request the following Android system permissions:

4.1 Network Permission

  • Permission Name: INTERNET, ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE
  • Purpose: Load and display advertising content
  • Note: Advertising services require network connection to fetch and display ads

4.2 Storage Permission (Optional)

  • Purpose: Save game statistics and user settings to local device storage
  • Note: This data is stored only on your device and is not uploaded to the network

4.3 Vibration Permission (If vibration feature is implemented)

  • Permission Name: VIBRATE
  • Purpose: Provide haptic feedback during specific game events (such as clicking on a mine)
  • Note: This permission is only used to enhance the gaming experience and does not involve any data collection

Important Note: Except for network permissions required by advertising services, the App does not request other sensitive permissions such as location, camera, microphone, or contacts.

5. Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party services:

5.1 Advertising Services

The App uses third-party advertising platforms to display ads. These advertising service providers may include (but are not limited to):

  • Google AdMob
  • Other advertising networks

These service providers have their own privacy policies that govern how they collect and use information. We recommend that you review the privacy policies of these service providers:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob Privacy Policy

5.2 Ad Personalization and Opt-Out

Advertising service providers may use your device's advertising ID to provide personalized ads. You can manage your ad preferences through the following methods:

  • Android Devices: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • Reset Advertising ID: Settings > Google > Ads > Reset Advertising ID

Important Note: Except for advertising services, the App does not integrate any other analytics tools or social media plugins.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for users of all ages. We do not actively collect personal information from any users (including children).

However, integrated advertising services may collect device information for displaying ads. In accordance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and other relevant laws, we recommend that parents supervise their children's use of this app.

If you are a parent and are concerned about advertising content, you can limit ad personalization in your device settings or use parental control features.

7. Data Security

The App itself does not collect or transmit any personal user data. All game data is stored locally on your device and is protected by your device's security measures.

For information collected by advertising services, this data is processed and protected by third-party advertising service providers. The advertising service providers we choose all follow industry-standard security measures to protect user information.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be communicated to you through app updates. We recommend that you review this Privacy Policy regularly to stay informed of the latest information.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

Email: minesweeper.game.support@example.com
